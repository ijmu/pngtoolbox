About PNGToolbox

What PNGToolbox does

PNGToolbox provides browser-based tools for converting WebP files to PNG and making simple PNG backgrounds transparent. The site focuses on practical image tasks for design assets, product photos, logos, icons, screenshots, and upload workflows.

Local processing

Image conversion and transparent background preview run in your browser. PNGToolbox does not require server-side uploads for normal tool use, which keeps common image conversions direct and private.

Content and guides

The guides explain when PNG is useful, how transparency works, and why WebP and PNG behave differently. They are written for users who need clear image files without installing design software.

Questions, corrections, and feedback can be sent through the contact page.
